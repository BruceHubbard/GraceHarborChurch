Inspire

Breakthrough, out-of-the-dodecahedron thinking is what we are about.

Incremental improvements are helpful, but breakthrough, out-of-the-dodecahedron thinking is what we are about. By applying scientific ideas and human insights uncovered through our research techniques, new connections are formed, existing assumptions are challenged, and innovative ideas are inspired.

We have developed creative and innovative solutions for use in environments as diverse as assistive habitats and factory floors. By incorporating the discipline of lean project management into our design process, Pomiet works closely with stakeholders to design a product that not only meets the needs of the market but also the needs of the user. Beginning with the earliest strategic and conceptual phases and extending through prototyping, our design process is iterative and human centered.

Collaboration

Creativity and innovation do not just happen. They are the result of hard work, collaboration, and feedback. At Pomiet, our iterative processes drive innovation yet are flexible enough to nurture great ideas. We have built-in feedback loops in each step of our design process. By incorporating client and user feedback into each iteration, we can quickly reach an optimal design.

Maximum Constraints Require Maximum Creativity

Significant constraints appear in many aspects of product development. Frustrated individuals are usually a sign of constraints that have not been properly addressed. The greater influence technology has on our day-to-day life, the greater this frustration grows. At Pomiet, we believe that creativity is fueled by these constraints. Our highly iterative approach helps to reveal the constraints of a product allowing for develop of optimal solutions.
