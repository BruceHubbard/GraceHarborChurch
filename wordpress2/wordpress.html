New to Grace Harbor?

We recognize how intimidating it can be to approach a church for the first time. We invite you to come in, grab some coffee, and take a look around. Our goal is not only that you would feel at home, but that you will be able to encounter God in a real and meaningful way through worship and teaching, connect with God’s people, and learn to serve Him in the world around us.
